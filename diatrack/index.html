Dia-Track

A diabetes monitoring web application designed to assit you in tracking your blood glucose levels,
exercise, medication, water and nutrition!

Visit us for more information

Features of the website

Scroll below to learn about the features Dia-Track offers.

Blood Glucose Record

On the Blood Glucose Tab, you can input your blood glucose reading on a favourable basis (Daily, Weekly, Monthly. The blood glucose levels will be measured in (mg/dl). After entry on the reading, clicking the create button, followed by the read button, post the reading on the table section. This allows for later review.

Exercise Record

On the Exercise Tab, you can record your exercise by entering the name and duration of the exercise. After entry of any record, the create button should be clicked to create the record, followed be the post button to add it to a table for later review.

Medication Record

On the Medication Tab, you can input your current medicines and the required dosage. This page will help serve as a reminder and help keep a log of you medication. after entering your medicine and dosage, clicking create button then the post button allows you to add it to the table below.

Nutrition Record

On the Nutrition Tab, you can input your water and carbohydrates intake on favorable basis (Daily, Weekly, Monthy). This page will help serve as a log and help keep a track of you nutrition. after entering your medicine and dosage, clicking create button then the post button allows you to add it to the table below.
